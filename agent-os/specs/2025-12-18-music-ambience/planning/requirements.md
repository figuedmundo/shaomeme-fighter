# Requirements: Music & Ambience

## Overview

Implement background music and UI sound effects to enhance the game's atmosphere and feedback. This includes stage-specific tracks, menu themes, dynamic music adjustments, and interface interaction sounds.

## Core Features

- **Stage-Specific Music**: Different upbeat, looping tracks for each arena.
- **Menu Music**: distinct themes for Title Screen and Character Select.
- **Dynamic Music**: Music speeds up or changes intensity at low health or during the final round.
- **UI Sounds**: Audio feedback for menu navigation, button presses, and selection confirmations.
